EXP is the final component of the phased development of Northeastern University’s new 600,000 GSF science/engineering precinct which includes the award-winning ISEC (completed in 2018), pedestrian bridge and landscaped plaza. It provides 357,000 GSF over 8 stories of science and engineering research space, with programmatic focus on science and engineering that builds on the University’s areas of excellence in cyber security, robotics and health. Spaces include robotics high bay, makerspace, classrooms and NU’s entire chemistry teaching program.
